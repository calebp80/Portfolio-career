About Me

My passion for public health began early in my undergraduate experience. I am a driven and creative force in my community. I recently graduated CSUN with a bachelors degree in Public Health, and am currently a candidate for the MPH at Claremont Graduate University with an emphasis on Epidemiology and Biostatistics. My work experience as of fall 2020 includes two epidemiological internships with LA County, and numerous extra curricular activities related to public health based in California with the California Youth Action Network (CYAN), the Clinton Foundation, the California State University, Northridge, Sigma Phi Epsilon, NSO, etc. I am currently looking for entry level work experience and/or internships that relate to data analysis. I have a keen interest in business and marketing, and am hoping to be approved for a dual degree from CGU combining my MPH with an MBA. Currently, my aspirations are to become a research epidemiologist for infectious diseases as well as the founder for my own non-profit organization that caters to communities of people coping with mental disorders within the psychosis spectrum also known as Students Understood by Coaches for Effective and Ethical Diagnosis (SUCEED). I have many goals, and the skills to get me where I want to be. I am quick-witted, focused, and determined to make my health philosophy a reality for underrepresented communities. Feel free to send me a message, I would love to expand my network, and continue to build my resume, and portfolio.
